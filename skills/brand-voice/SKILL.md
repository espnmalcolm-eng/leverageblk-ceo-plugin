# LeverageBLK brand voice

LeverageBLK is a trust-first professional network for Black professionals. Premium,
curated, relationship-led. The voice reflects that: considered, warm, and confident
without being loud.

## Voice principles

- **Premium, not corporate.** Editorial and intentional — a well-made magazine, not a
  SaaS pitch. Full sentences, real rhythm, no jargon scaffolding.
- **Earned, not hyped.** Access and reputation in the product are earned; the language
  should feel the same. No growth-hack urgency, no manufactured scarcity theater.
- **From inside the culture.** Speak as a member of the community, not a brand observing
  it. Cultural fluency over cultural reference.
- **Restraint signals quality.** One strong idea, well said, beats a list of five.
  White space is a feature.
- **Trust is the throughline.** Everything ladders back to real connections and real
  leverage — the tagline is the north star, not a slogan to repeat.

## Words and moves to avoid

- Corporate filler: "unlock", "seamless", "empower", "leverage" (as a verb), "elevate", "supercharge".
- Growth-hack energy: "Don't miss out", "Limited spots!!", countdns, fake urgency.
- Exclamation-mark tone. Let the idea carry the weight.
- Generic DEI-speak or performative language. Specificity and respect over slogans.
- The old "Vouch" language. It's **Co-Sign**, **The Tap**, **The Room** now.

## Product language (use exactly)

- **Co-Sign** — public endorsement, 5 per user for life.
- **The Tap** — private reputation signal.
- **The Room** — 90-day warm-intro network access.
- **Daily Nuggets** — daily networking wisdom in-app.
- **Locate** — geospatial discovery of nearby professionals.

## Visual system (for Canva, decks, social assets)

Keep every asset matching the app and landing page:

- **Palette:** Ink `#0A0A0A` (base) · Gold `#D4B86A` (primary accent, emphasis only) ·
  Terracotta `#C26B4F` (governance/community) · Emerald `#4A7A5C` (live/active) ·
  Ivory `#F4EFE3` (light type on dark).
- **Type:** Fraunces (serif display, one headline moment per asset) · Plus Jakarta Sans
  (body/UI) · JetBrains Mono (eyebrows, labels, metrics).
- **Rule:** dark backgrounds, gold used sparingly for emphasis, generous space,
  max one serif headline per piece. Gold is never wallpaper.

## When reviewing content

Check in this order: (1) is the product language correct? (2) does it read premium and
earned, not hyped? (3) is it one clear idea? (4) do the visuals match the palette/type
rules? Flag anything off before it goes out.
