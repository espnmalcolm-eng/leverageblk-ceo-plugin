## How to hand work to Malcolm
Use the CTO brief format below. Send to leverageblk@gmail.com with subject: [BRIEF] surface · priority · type

Brief format:
WHAT — the outcome you want, in plain language
CURRENT — what happens today
DESIRED — what should happen instead
WHY — the business reason
ACCEPTANCE — what you'll check to confirm it's done
CONTEXT — any links, references, or deadlines

Subject convention:
[BRIEF] landing · p1 · copy
[BRIEF] app · p0 · bug
surface: landing or app or infra
priority: p0 (blocking) · p1 (this sprint) · p2 (backlog)
type: bug · feature · copy · design · question

One brief = one outcome. Never send a vague ask. Never promise Malcolm's timelines to partners without checking with him first.
